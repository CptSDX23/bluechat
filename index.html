BlueChat Log In

A simple JS chat application

Nothing fancy, just accounts and chatrooms. Set whitelists and blacklists for your servers to customize who's let in, and keep a log of all messages sent in a server. No data limits and linking accounts, just enter a username and password and you're ready to join a server.

About BlueChat

BlueChat is made by CptSDX23 and is currently in its 0.0.5 version. It is just a simple side project, so don't expect too frequent/substantial updates. Also, this web app is extremely insecure, so don't share personal information anywhere on this site.
